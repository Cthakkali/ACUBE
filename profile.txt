COMPANY PROFILE A-CUBE INSTITUTE OF SKILLS
Company Overview
A-Cube Institute of Skills, incorporated in 2021, is a leading human resources supply and skill development solutions provider committed to empowering individuals and supporting organizations through quality manpower and workforce development services.
With over 5 years of combined industry experience, A-Cube Institute of Skills has established itself as a trusted partner for government departments, corporate organizations, CSR initiatives, and development projects across Tamil Nadu. The organization specializes in manpower supply, recruitment services, skill development, training implementation, and human resource consulting.
Our commitment to quality, reliability, and client satisfaction has enabled us to build long-term relationships with various organizations while contributing to employment generation and workforce development.

Major Projects Executed
Skill Development & CSR Projects
As an Official Training Partner of the Apparel, Made-Ups & Home Furnishing Sector Skill Council (AMHSSC), A-Cube Institute of Skills has successfully implemented numerous skill development and livelihood enhancement projects across Tamil Nadu.
Key Achievements
•	Trained and certified over 2,000 candidates in apparel sector job roles.
•	Successfully implemented multiple CSR-funded skill development initiatives focused on women empowerment and sustainable livelihoods.
•	Facilitated employment opportunities for trained women candidates through industry linkages and placement support.
•	Delivered industry-aligned training programs with assessment and certification support under recognized skill development frameworks.

Major CSR Projects
New Space India Limited (NSIL) CSR Initiative
Successfully implemented a large-scale skill development project in Raichur, Karnataka, benefiting 400 candidates. The project focused on enhancing employability and livelihood opportunities through industry-relevant vocational training, certification, and placement support.
Chennai Petroleum Corporation Limited (CPCL) CSR Project
Implemented a skill development and livelihood enhancement project in North Chennai, covering the areas of Villivakam, Washermen pet, Manali, and Padi. The project is designed to train 300 women and youth in apparel sector job roles, promoting sustainable employment and economic empowerment.
Antrix Corporation Limited CSR Initiative
Successfully implemented skill development programs in Bagalkot and Raichur, Karnataka, benefiting 300 candidates. The initiative focused on vocational skill enhancement, workforce readiness, and creating employment opportunities for disadvantaged youth and women.
Overall Impact
•	Trained and certified more than 2,000 candidates in apparel sector job roles.
•	Implemented CSR-funded livelihood and workforce development projects across Tamil Nadu and Karnataka.
•	Created sustainable employment opportunities for women through industry partnerships and placement facilitation.
•	Successfully delivered projects in partnership with leading public sector organizations and CSR stakeholders.
